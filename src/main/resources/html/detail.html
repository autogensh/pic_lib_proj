Egg Chair Loungechair by Fritz Hansen

PRO

3d model Egg Chair Loungechair designed by Arne Jacobsen in 1958 for Fritz Hansen. Fritz Hansen started designing furniture in 1872 and the company partnered with Arne Jacobsen in 1934. The collaboration resulted in designs way ahead of their time and the label created furniture for the Copenhagen City Hall and The Supreme Court, among others. Dimension of the chair: H 107 x L 86 x D 95 cm. The vray and corona file comes with materials in place, ready to render it.
